Probabilities

    Definitions

    • P(Swing): Probability the batter swings on the next pitch.
    • P(Contact): Probability of contact given a swing.
    • P(Whiff): Probability of whiff (miss) given a swing.
    • P(Foul): Probability of foul given a swing.
    • P(Ball): Probability the pitch is called a ball.
    • P(Strike): Probability the pitch is called a strike (including swinging/called).
    • P(Hit): Probability the pitch results in a hit.
    • P(Out): Probability the pitch results in an out.
    • P(Struck Out): Probability the pitch results in a strikeout.
    • P(Home Run): Probability the pitch results in a home run.
    • P(Extra Base Hit): Probability the pitch results in a double, triple, or home run.
    • P(In Play): Probability the pitch is put in play.
    • P(Walk): Probability the pitch results in a walk (BB).
    • P(Hit By Pitch): Probability the pitch results in a hit by pitch (HBP).
    • P(Error): Probability the pitch results in a fielding error.
    • P(Sacrifice): Probability the pitch results in a sacrifice (fly or bunt).
    • Statistically Relevant: Indicates if sample size (total pitches) is sufficient (≥50) for reliable stats.
    • For expanded matches: base_state format is 'on_1b-on_2b-on_3b' (e.g., '0-1-0' for man on 2nd).
